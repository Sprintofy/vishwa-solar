FAQs

Everything You Need To Know About Solar Energy

What is Solar Energy and how does it work?

Solar energy converts sunlight into electricity using photovoltaic panels. When sunlight hits the solar cells, it creates an electrical current that powers your home or business, reducing your reliance on grid electricity.

How much can I save with solar panels?

Our customers typically save up to 90% on their electricity bills. Savings depend on your system size, energy consumption, and local electricity rates. Most systems pay for themselves within 3-6 years.

What is the difference between on-grid and hybrid systems?

On-grid systems connect to the electricity grid for net metering. Hybrid systems include battery backup for power during outages. We help you choose based on your location and power reliability needs.

Do I qualify for government subsidies?

Yes! Residential customers can avail central government subsidies of up to 40% on system costs. We assist with all paperwork and subsidy applications to maximize your benefits.

What warranties come with the solar system?

Mahindra solar panels come with 25-year performance warranty. Inverters have 5-10 year warranties. We also provide workmanship warranty on installation and ongoing maintenance support.

How long does installation take?

Typical residential installations take 2-3 days. Commercial projects may take longer depending on size. We provide a detailed timeline during the proposal stage.

What maintenance is required?

Solar systems require minimal maintenance. Regular cleaning of panels (2-4 times yearly) and annual inspections are recommended. We offer maintenance packages for hassle-free operation.

Will solar work during monsoon or cloudy days?

Yes! Modern panels generate electricity even on cloudy days, though at reduced capacity. Annual production accounts for seasonal variations. Hybrid systems with batteries ensure uninterrupted power.
