Solar On-Grid System

Connects directly to your electricity meter and helps save up to 90% on your electricity bills. Perfect for homes and offices with reliable grid connectivity. Supports net metering, allowing you to earn credits for excess power generation.

Solar Hybrid System

Best for areas with frequent power cuts. Works with battery backup to provide uninterrupted power supply even when grid power is off. Combines the benefits of both on-grid and off-grid systems for maximum reliability.

Solar Decorative Lighting

Complete range of solar lighting solutions including brick lights for gardens and parking, bollard lights for open spaces and terraces, and street lights for roads, societies, and farm areas. Energy-efficient and maintenance-free outdoor lighting.
